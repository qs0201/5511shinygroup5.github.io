Poisson Distribution App Overview:

This website provides three different apps for learning the Poisson distribution.


The Three Apps:

The App 1 is designed to demonstrate the small difference in probabilities between Poisson and Binomial distribution when the number of intervals n is big. Theorectically, Poisson distribution is a limiting case of Binomial distribution.

The App 2 is designed to demonstrate how Poisson probability mass function can be applied in calculating

  1. the probability of an event occuring k times
  2. the probability of an event rehappening within t minutes

The App 3 is designed to provide students opportunity to visualize the graphical representation of Poisson distribution. This App aims at

  1. assisting students to calculate the probability P( X < k ) under Poisson distribution ,
  2. observing the key features of Poisson Distribution graphs.
